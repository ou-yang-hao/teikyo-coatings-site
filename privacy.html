Information we collect

When you submit an inquiry, the website may collect your name, company, work email, phone number, country or region, application interest and project description.

How we use it

The information is used to review your request, contact you, prepare technical or commercial responses and maintain a record of the conversation.

Storage and access

Inquiry data is stored in the website database. Access should be limited to authorized personnel and protected through appropriate operational and technical controls.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of your inquiry data by contacting the email address shown on this website.
